His top 3 works

Movie #1 Willy Wonka & the Chocolate Factory

Summary

A young downtrodden boy by the name Charlie through luck and persistence comes upon a Golden Ticket. This leads him to an eccentric chocolate maker's factory played by Gene Wilder who shows Charlie the world of possibilities while learning a little something himself.

Movie #2 Young Frankenstein

Summary

In a movie ode to the classic Frankenstein premise we recieve an fresh take on the story with comedic undertones. Shot in black and white with parody and satire, Wilder's performance in this is sure to surprise and please.

Movie #3 Blazing Sadles

Summary

Two men find themselves caught up in funny but topical shenanigans set in the wild west period. This story about brotherhood and virtue is a cult classic for good reason. Timeless and charming this movie acted as a necessary bridge and commentary into the modern culture of today.
